Open PhD positions

Nov 22, 2021

We have PhD positions for Fall 2022. If you are interested in shaping the future of materials, soft robotics, haptics, or assistive technology with us apply to CMU's School of Computer Science.

We are building an interdisciplinary team and are looking for candidates with an interest in HCI and skills in computer graphics (geometry processing, physics simulation, inverse design) and/or mechanical design (fabrication, compliant mechanisms, hands-on building).

Find all information and deadlines on the admissions pages of HCII or the School of Computer Science. You can be in any department and be a core member of the Interactive Structures lab. If you have any questions, reach out to Prof. Alexandra Ion.
