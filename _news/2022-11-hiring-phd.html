Open PhD positions

Oct 31, 2022

We have PhD positions for Fall 2023. We are looking for enthusiastic students with strong technical skills and interest in topics such as metamaterials, digital fabrication, geometry processing, physics simulation, soft robotics, or haptics. If you are interested in working with us, reach out to Prof. Alexandra Ion or other team members you would like to chat with.

To apply, find all information and deadlines on the admissions pages of HCII or the School of Computer Science. You can be in any department and be a core member of the Interactive Structures lab.
